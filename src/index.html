About me

Hello there, I'm Juan. A recent graduate of Hack Reactor and Full Stack Developer with the drive to learn and develop myself. In August of 2003 Myspace was deployed to the world, and like many kids of the time I was captivated with the possibilities provided by the sites HTML and CSS options. Ever since then I knew I wanted to become a Developer, but I was never quite able to make that dream come true, that is until recently. I have had the opportunity to reawaken a passion of mine while learning technologies 10 year old me would have never imagined. From refining HTML and CSS to learning Javascript, JSX, Tachyon, React, Hooks and Redux for the front end along with Python, Django, PostgreSQL, Fast and Resftfull API'sr for the backend, I have developed the skills necessary to become a productive member of any team I'm in.

When not on a keyboard I enjoy reading a good mystery or fantasy book.
