About me

Greetings, I'm Juan, a recent graduate of Hack Reactor and a dedicated Full Stack Developer committed to continuous growth and advancement. My journey into the realm of development began with the emergence of Myspace in August 2003, sparking my fascination with the creative possibilities offered by HTML and CSS. Since then, I've harbored a profound desire to delve deeper into the world of software development, a dream that I have now realized. Through relentless dedication, I've reignited my passion and ventured into technologies that far surpass what my younger self could have envisioned. I've honed my skills, transitioning from refining HTML and CSS to mastering a spectrum of tools such as Javascript, JSX, Tachyon, React, Hooks, and Redux for frontend development. These experiences have equipped me with the expertise necessary to contribute effectively to any team, combining my frontend finesse with a robust backend skill set to drive innovation and deliver impactful solutions."
